HitPoints

Hit Points was a branding project in my final year of undergrad. This is only a snippet of the branding made for it, but its my favorite pieces of the project. This took a large part of the semester, about 80% of it. I wanted to create a brand thats focus was bringing in eductaional content to the esports scene. The focus was on how to better oneself in playing, coaching, or advertising esports, using content generated by the online community. To motivate others to create content, Hit Points would send out gear to help in content creation, hence the packaging and letterhead.

This project was a harder one for me, since I am used to very fast design work. I had to force myself to slow down and really process each choice. This was also the first time I used photoshop in order to modify mock-ups. Overall, this project was challenging and I learned a lot from it, but I don't know if timespans of multiple months are my area of focus. I much prefer the short term.

3 Color Stickers

Believe it or not, all of these stickers were made using only 3 colors. In fact, its the three colors I mainly used on this website, #2C363F, #EF7171, and #FBD87F. This project was to create a sticker sheet using only 3 colors, but there were no limitations on layer styles. So, to render my characters cohesively but in their own colors, I simply would layer different colors and layer styles until I liked the result. This project was a great one for me to push the guidelines a bit and really see what I could do to adapt to limited colors.

Each character is one I designed. In order, there is Phoebe, Myros, Catreia, Cynder, Seirilith, and Amber. I chose these 6 based on close ties to aspects of my own personality and identity.
