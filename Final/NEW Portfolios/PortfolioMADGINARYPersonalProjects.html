Fairy Globe

This piece came from no where. I had no class or project in mind when making this, but it just happened to be what I drew that particular day. I go back to this piece a lot, as it was the start of my current style. I still alter some stuff as I go and create, but this piece was a foundation to me and my style.

Doll Study

Part of my hobbies is doll collecting. One series I collect has very unique clothing per doll, so to get better at absract and wild clothing, I decided to draw each of my dolls as a study. I have 6 of the 19 done, but at the time of this specific drawing, I only owned 6. I really enjoy how this doll line plays with color, and using a real life object in-the-round as a reference rather than images.

Winx Club Tarot Deck

I personally am a Pagan, and read tarot. I wanted to make a major arcana only deck of the Winx Club, a show I used to watch as a kid. This sparked a lot of my intrest in magic, and fantasy in general. I use the form that, in the show, is earned via saving someone or multiple people from their home planet. I did this for an intention of protection and guardianship to be placed into the deck as I drew it.

I have a list of what character represents which card, with reasons as to why I chose each. For example, Bloom is the Magician due to her origin story, and Flora is the Empress based on her growth in character and skill.

Poison Type Personified

One of the on going projects I use to prevent art block is drawing each Pokemon type as a person and representing underrepresented people as I do. This was the first work in the series. I aimed for a more urban art feel of poison, and less so into the pollution type like the Pokemon series does. I wanted to also experiment with wild colors, like toxic frogs and flowers in the wild.
